Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes, 100% free. We may offer optional premium features in the future (like SMS alerts), but basic email reminders will always be free.

How do you have my license info?

Texas license data is public record, available from TDLR. We simply make it more useful by adding reminders.

Will you spam me?

No. You'll only receive renewal reminders and occasional relevant updates. Unsubscribe anytime.

Do you handle the actual renewal?

No, we're a reminder service. We provide direct links to TDLR for the actual renewal process.
